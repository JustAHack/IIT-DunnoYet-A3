    • Team Processes and Communication

    How will your group communicate

    Being a team of 2 allows communications to be simpler. Using MS Teams to collaborate our work and posting updates. Further the use of Trello to keep updated on progress. We also use Discord for instant messaging.

    Meetings

    Meetings to take place twice a week via MS Teams, recorded with video. Planned meetings every Tuesday and Friday at 10pm, with unwritten agreement if either member is unavailable, meeting to be held at earliest convenience.

    Communication Expectation

    Regular interactions, generally daily, regarding what we have achieved and any questions or confirmations, usually in Discord. Major announcements in Teams.

    Team Processess

    If a member is unable to complete a task, do any work etc due to being unwell or unavailable they must communicate this to the team with a post on MS Teams or Discord assuring that at least one team member receives the notification and responds.

    If a member has issues with another member they must solve the issue as soon as possible within a professional manner, preferably personally and not in the open in front of other team members. If a resolution cannot be reached, they should reach out to the lecturing staff to try and reach a solution with their assistance.

    Group reflection

    Group:

    What went well

    The creation of our prototype, we had originally believed we would have to create a prototype in Figma of what the user interface might be, however we released we had enough time to build a prototype in code that would run on a web server and provide the functionality of calculating wages from a form that recorded shift data. This has brought us closer to our personal goals and helped develop team building skills for our future
